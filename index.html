How to Play

  1. Start the Game: Click on the start figure to begin your journey through Shrek's labyrinth.
  2. Navigate the Maze: Carefully move your mouse to guide the cursor through the maze. The path is intricate, and precision is key.
  3. Avoid the Walls: Touching the walls of the maze results in an immediate loss. You will need to start over from the beginning of the level.
  4. Progress Through Levels: The game consists of three levels, each increasing in complexity and difficulty.
  5. Complete All Levels: Successfully navigate through all three levels to reach the end of the game.
